C-Dawg’s Snack Shack – California Cottage Food Bakery

C-Dawg’s Snack Shack is a California cottage food bakery based in Escondido, serving customers throughout California with handmade cookies, nostalgic treats, Rice Krispy treats, sourdough, jams, bars, and small-batch baked goods.

Every product is made in small batches with quality ingredients and a focus on bold, nostalgic, feel-good flavors. C-Dawg’s Snack Shack specializes in cookies and treats that feel homemade, giftable, and celebration-ready.

Small-Batch Cookies Made in California

Our cookie lineup includes classic bakery favorites and playful modern flavors, including chocolate chip cookies, brown butter chocolate chip cookies, sprinkle sugar cookies, Nanny’s Pound Cake cookies, Mama Z’s Monster cookies, white chocolate macadamia nut cookies, ginger molasses cookies, red velvet cookies, oatmeal raisin cookies, and peanut butter cookies.

Rice Krispy Treats, Bars, Sourdough, Jams, and More

In addition to cookies, C-Dawg’s Snack Shack offers extra gooey Rice Krispy treats, handmade bars, sourdough, jams, seasonal sweets, and nostalgic desserts. Product availability may change as fresh batches and seasonal items are added.

California Cottage Food Shipping

C-Dawg’s Snack Shack operates under California Cottage Food Law. Products are available for shipping within California only. Orders placed with shipping addresses outside California may be canceled to remain compliant with state cottage food regulations.
